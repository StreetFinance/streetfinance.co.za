About Streetlight

We treat your business as if it were our own.

As the name suggests, Streetlight Financial Services lights the way for small and medium businesses — our main offering a full accounting function, tailored specifically for the SME market.

That means access to a complete accounting department without the high cost of hiring and administering the professionals yourself. You get peace of mind that the financial side is handled, while you concentrate on the core business.

What we do

One team for the whole finance function.

Pick the pieces you need today, add more as you grow. Every package is a variation of our full accounting offering.

Bookkeeping

Maintenance of your monthly accounting records, captured and reconciled.

Management accounts

Preparation & analysis of monthly management accounts you can actually read.

CIPC compliance

We keep your company compliant in terms of CIPC — annual returns and filings.

Tax services

Individuals, companies & trusts — income tax, VAT, dividends and employee taxes, calculated and submitted.

Payroll

Administering your monthly payroll on Sage or Simple Pay.

Registrations

Company, VAT, PAYE, Workman's Comp and Department of Labour registrations — handled end to end.

Advisory access

Access to professionals in the field on an advisory basis, whenever you need them.

Workman's Comp

Compliance with the Workman's Compensation Fund — registration and returns.
